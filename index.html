Cost Description
Comparison is based on marginal cost of operating an existing coal plant (Coal MC) to the levelized cost of electicity (LCOE) of a new utility-scale solar PV installation. The infrastructure cost of coal plants are not included in the Coal MC since replacing coal plants does not return the infrastructure cost.
Coal MC[$/MWh] = (Fuel cost [$/yr]+O&M cost[$/yr] + Carbon tax[$/yr])/Annual generation [MWh/yr]
Costs due to emissions for NOx and SO2 are highly variable in recent years. However, as more coal plants have retired, the price of these allowances has decreased. Therefore, we neglected these costs on our calculations.
